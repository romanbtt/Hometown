Top three activities to do in Montreal

Old Montreal Stroll

Enjoy Old Montreal's cobblestone streets, historic landmarks like Notre-Dame Basilica, and the lively atmosphere of Place Jacques-Cartier.

Mount Royal Hike

Ascend Mount Royal for panoramic views of Montreal's skyline, choosing between stairs or scenic trails for a rewarding experience and stunning vistas.

Montreal Museum Visit

Explore art at the Montreal Museum of Fine Arts, featuring a diverse collection from classic paintings to contemporary exhibits, providing a captivating journey through various artistic styles.
